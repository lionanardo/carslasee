Welcome to Every Day Auto

Where passion for classic cars meets a commitment to preserving automotive history. Founded in 2005 by Frank "Rusty" Reynolds, our company is built on a lifelong love for vintage vehicles and a desire to share that passion with fellow enthusiasts.

Meet Our Founder

Frank "Rusty" Reynolds is the heart and soul of Every Day Auto. Growing up in the 1960s, Rusty spent countless weekends tinkering with his father’s classic muscle cars, dreaming of the day he could restore one to its former glory. After a successful career in engineering, he decided to turn his passion into a business, creating a haven for classic car lovers.

At 75, Rusty remains actively involved in the garage, sharing stories of the iconic cars he has restored and inspiring the next generation of car enthusiasts. His journey is a testament to the power of following one’s passion and the joy of keeping classic cars alive.

A Defining Moment

One of Rusty's most memorable experiences was discovering a rare 1969 Ford Mustang in a barn during a road trip. Covered in dust and rust, the car was a hidden gem waiting to be restored. Rusty negotiated with the owner and brought the Mustang back to Every Day Auto, where he and his team dedicated months to restoring it to its original condition. This experience exemplifies our commitment to uncovering and revitalizing classic vehicles.

Community Impact

At Every Day Auto, we believe in giving back to the community. Rusty initiated a program that offers workshops for young people interested in automotive restoration. This initiative not only teaches valuable skills but also fosters a love for classic cars among the youth. As Rusty often says, "Every car has a story, and it's our job to keep those stories alive."

Our Legacy

As Every Day Auto continues to thrive, Rusty's legacy is evident in every car that rolls out of our shop. His passion for muscle cars and vintage vehicles is not just a business; it’s a way of life that inspires everyone around him. At Every Day Auto, we are more than just a classic car dealership; we are guardians of automotive history, dedicated to ensuring that the beauty of classic cars is appreciated for generations to come.

Join us on this journey, and let’s keep the stories of classic cars alive together!
