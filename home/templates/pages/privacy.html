Privacy Policy for Every Day Auto Sales

At Every Day Auto Sales (“we,” “us,” or “our”), we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you interact with our website, communicate with us, or purchase a vehicle.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We gather personal details that you voluntarily provide, including your name, address, phone number, email address, and financial information, when you:
    • Contact us via our website, email, or phone.
    • Request a quote or schedule a test drive.
    • Apply for financing.
    • Purchase a vehicle.
    • Subscribe to our mailing list.
  • Usage Information: We automatically collect data about your interactions with our website, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring pages, and browsing behavior, to optimize our website and improve user experience.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to gather information about your browsing activities and preferences. You can modify your browser settings to block cookies, but this may affect certain website functionalities.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide requested services, such as vehicle quotes, test drives, and financing options.
  • Respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests.
  • Process purchases and transactions.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Send marketing communications if you have opted in.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers that support our business, such as marketing services, website hosting, and financial institutions.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or in response to lawful requests from law enforcement or regulatory authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

Protecting Your Information

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no online platform is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by following the unsubscribe link in any of our communications.
  • Access and Modify Your Information: You may request access to or updates of your personal information by contacting us.
  • Delete Your Information: You can request the deletion of your personal data by reaching out to us using the contact details provided below.

Contact Us

Updates to This Privacy Policy
